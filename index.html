Jaleshworee Secondary School

Shree Jaleshworee Secondary School is a renowned educational institution located in Siddhelek-4, Salanghat, Dhading. Known as one of the best government schools in the area, it provides quality education and fosters a nurturing environment for students. The school is committed to academic excellence and holistic development, aiming to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for their future success.
